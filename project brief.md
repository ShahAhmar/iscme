ISCME 2026 Conference Website - Development Brief
Project Overview
Develop a brand-new modern conference website for ISCME 2026 under the GAFTIM ecosystem.
This is NOT a clone of the previous website. The design should be completely fresh and modern while temporarily reusing content from previous conference websites (BiTi & ISCME) until new content is provided.
The website should look comparable to leading international conference websites such as:
•	CES 
•	LEAP 
•	GITEX 
•	Web Summit 
•	MWC Barcelona 
•	VivaTech 
The goal is to make GAFTIM conferences look world-class.
