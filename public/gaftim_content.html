About GAFTIM

GAFTIM (Global Academic Forum on Technology, Innovation, and Management) stands as a beacon of excellence in the realms of academic publishing and research collaboration. Dedicated to advancing the frontiers of technology, innovation, and management, GAFTIM publishes international journals and global scholarly works, ensuring rigorous peer-reviewed standards. Through its prestigious platforms such as Scopus, Web of Science (WoS), IEEE, Springer, Emerald, Sage, and IGI Publications, GAFTIM provides unparalleled visibility and impact. The organization also spearheads international conferences, fostering strategic partnerships and industry collaboration to bridge academia and real-world applications. Its research center is a hub for pioneering research projects and securing research grants. GAFTIM is equally committed to digital transformation and capacity building, offering expert consultancy, consultation services, and comprehensive training sessions. By mentoring in academic, research, and scholarly endeavors, GAFTIM empowers researchers and professionals with technological solutions and innovative approaches, solidifying its role as a pivotal entity in global academic and industry landscapes.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To exchange knowledge that leads to progress and prosperity in society through excellence in technology, innovation, and management research.

Mission

The Global Academic Forum on Technology, Innovation, and Management (GAFTIM) works diligently to exchange knowledge that fosters societal progress and prosperity through excellence in technology, innovation, and management research. GAFTIM aims to be a renowned knowledge channel through its international conferences, academic journals, and consultancy services.

Main Goals
  1. Promote Academic Excellence: Publish peer-reviewed international journals and global scholarly works to advance knowledge in technology, innovation, and management.
  2. Foster Research Collaboration: Facilitate collaborative research projects and secure research grants to support cutting-edge research initiatives.
  3. Organize International Conferences: Host global conferences, symposia, and forums that connect thought leaders, researchers, and professionals.
  4. Enhance Visibility: Ensure publications are indexed in prestigious databases such as Scopus, Web of Science, IEEE, Springer, Emerald, Sage, and IGI.
  5. Support Digital Transformation: Pioneer innovative strategies in digital transformation to shape the future of academic and industry landscapes.
  6. Cultivate Strategic Partnerships: Build strategic partnerships with leading industries to apply academic research to real-world applications.
  7. Provide Expert Consultancy: Offer consultation services and training sessions in technology, innovation, and management to empower individuals and organizations.
  8. Mentor Future Leaders: Act as academic, research, and scholarly mentors to nurture the next generation of leaders in technology and innovation.
Core Values
  1. Research as a Core Competency: GAFTIM believes in the crucial role of research in generating new knowledge and understanding, which drives innovation and subsequently creates wealth.
  2. Collaboration: Committed to supporting teamwork, GAFTIM believes that individuals are more effective when they collaborate openly with groups.
  3. Diversity: GAFTIM ensures the best practices to respect and appreciate differences in ethnicity, gender, age, physical abilities, education, and religion among individuals and institutions.
  4. Learning Orientation: GAFTIM emphasizes continuous learning and knowledge sharing as fundamental to fostering innovation and holds everyone responsible for this process.
  5. Commitment: GAFTIM is dedicated to serving the academic community with activities that promote growth and prosperity.
